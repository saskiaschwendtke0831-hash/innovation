    Executive Core Findings

    Your leadership is strongly committed to integrating AI, but frontline enablement and formal data structures are limiting scaled rollouts. Starting with focused, low-risk pilots in HR policy response and Customer Service automation will yield high visibility and establish an operational playbook before tackling supply chain algorithms.

    Governance & Trust Center

    Corporate guardrails, audit standards, and risk monitoring frameworks.

    AI Usage Policy Guardrails

    All AI systems deployed within our retail structure must strictly follow three operational axioms:

    A
    Zero Data Leakage
    No customer records or vendor pricing shared with public model systems.
    B
    Human Verification Loop
    Any generative copy, code, or customer interaction draft requires human sign-off before launch.
    C
    Algorithm Transparency
    No black-box models used for customer demographic profiling.
